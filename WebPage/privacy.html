Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AimGame (“we”, “our” or “the App”) collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the AimGame mobile application (the “App”) on Android devices. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Data

  • Game Settings & Progress
    High scores, current level, sound on/off preferences and other gameplay settings are stored locally on your device in Android’s SharedPreferences. This data is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party.

1.2 Advertising & Analytics Data

AimGame integrates Google AdMob to display ads. To serve personalized ads, AdMob may collect:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) and related device identifiers
  • Device & OS characteristics (model, OS version, language, screen resolution)
  • IP address (handled and stored by Google’s servers)
  • Basic ad-viewing events (e.g. which ads you saw, when you tapped an ad)

All ad and analytics data collection is governed by Google’s own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your Advertising ID or IP address ourselves.

2. How We Use Information

  • Local Preferences & Progress
    To restore your game session, settings, and high scores when you relaunch the App.
  • Advertisements
    To request and display ads via AdMob.
